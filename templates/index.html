Frequently asked questions

What is HEIC?

HEIC is a modern image format commonly used by iPhones. It saves space, but some websites and systems still require JPG or PNG.

Is free conversion lower quality?

Free standard quality is designed for everyday use. Pro high/maximum quality gives more control for professional batches and important deliveries.

Do you store my images?

Files are processed temporarily for conversion. This service is designed not to intentionally store uploaded images after the download is generated.

Why are there limits?

Limits keep the free converter fast and protect the service from abuse. Larger batches and high quality are available in Pro plans.
